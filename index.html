Background

I am a Partner at Boston Consulting Group, where I lead engagements in Corporate Finance & Strategy and Health Care. My work focuses on growth strategy, total shareholder return, innovation, and large-scale transformation — primarily with MedTech, BioPharma, and Life Sciences companies.

I am a co-author of BCG's Most Innovative Companies and Vitality reports and bring considerable expertise in digital health, molecular diagnostics, pharmacology, data analytics, deep tech, and commercialization.

Before joining BCG, I was a research fellow in pharmacology at Baylor College of Medicine and a founder and board member of Enventure, a non-profit connecting life science entrepreneurs with educational and networking resources.
